Boot Scootin' Boogie

Beginner 32 Count 4 Wall
🎵 Music: Boot Scootin' Boogie – Brooks & Dunn 💃 Choreographer: Unknown

Section 1 – Grapevine Right

1-2: Step right, left crosses behind right
3-4: Step right, touch left beside right

Section 2 – Grapevine Left

5-6: Step left, right crosses behind left
7-8: Step left, touch right beside left

Section 3 – Walks Forward & Kick

9-10: Walk forward right, walk forward left
11-12: Walk forward right, kick left forward

Section 4 – Walks Back & Touch

13-14: Walk back left, walk back right
15-16: Walk back left, touch right beside left
